Left Leaning Template

Left template is a 'block style' page designed to be a 'text heavy' template that'll let you throw lots of high quality content up on the page in a way that is clean, legible, and easy to navigate.


It focuses on big and juicy 'grab my attention' spaces that let you throw up content without the normal concern for how large volumes of text tend to bore the user.
