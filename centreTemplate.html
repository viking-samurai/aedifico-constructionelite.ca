  • Our Approach to custom Home Additions

  • Complete Design and Construction for Additions to Existing Homes

    Expanding your home is a significant investment, and we make it a rewarding experience. Our Home Additions service is designed to seamlessly integrate new living spaces with your existing structure. Here's how we do it:

    • Conceptualization: We collaborate with you to conceptualize the ideal addition, ensuring it complements your current home's style and meets your needs.
    • Architectural Design: Our architects create detailed plans, seamlessly blending the new addition with your existing space. We focus on continuity, aesthetics, and functionality.
    • Construction Expertise: Our experienced builders execute the project with precision, minimizing disruption to your daily life. We maintain a clean and safe work environment throughout.
    • Quality Assurance: Like our Custom Home Building, we maintain the highest standards of craftsmanship and quality in every aspect of your addition.
    • Code Compliance: We ensure that all additions meet local building codes and regulations, providing peace of mind and compliance with legal requirements.

  • With Aedifico Construction Elite, you have a partner dedicated to making your dream additions a reality. We combine innovation, craftsmanship, and a commitment to your satisfaction in every project.
